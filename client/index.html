It's an emulator within the browser that has its input shared with people within the same room as you

Join a room or create one, invite people (i hope), load your own ROM (so does everyone individually), click ready, once everyone is ready you can start the game

If you input the button A, then you will be also inputing A in your roomates emulators, same goes for any GBA input

Imperfections due to the latency and different RNG will causes desync and you will have to cooperate to resync or deal with it until you can somewhat resync mutual situations

it should work on desktop and phones but i don't know if gamepads are functionnal
